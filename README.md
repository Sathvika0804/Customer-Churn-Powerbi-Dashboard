# 📊 Customer Churn Analysis & Power BI Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing customer churn using data analysis and visualization techniques. The goal is to identify key factors influencing customer churn and provide actionable business insights.

An interactive 5-page Power BI dashboard was developed to explore customer behavior, revenue impact, segmentation, and risk patterns.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Identify key drivers of customer churn  
- Analyze customer behavior and service usage  
- Evaluate revenue loss due to churn  
- Segment customers based on risk levels  
- Provide actionable business recommendations  

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn)  
- Power BI  
- Data Cleaning & Feature Engineering  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Pages

### 1️⃣ Customer Churn Overview
- Churn distribution  
- Churn by contract, tenure, and internet type  

### 2️⃣ Customer Behavior & Service Usage Analysis
- Engagement level impact  
- Streaming services and service usage patterns  
- Online security and support analysis  

### 3️⃣ Revenue Impact & Customer Value Analysis
- Revenue lost due to churn  
- High-value customer analysis  
- Monthly charges and spending patterns  

### 4️⃣ Customer Segmentation & Risk Insights
- Risk segmentation using tenure and contract  
- Customer grouping patterns  
- Payment behavior and churn  

### 5️⃣ Key Insights & Business Recommendations
- Identification of churn drivers  
- Strategic recommendations to reduce churn  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Customers with month-to-month contracts have the highest churn rate  
- New customers are more likely to churn  
- Low engagement significantly increases churn risk  
- Higher monthly charges are associated with higher churn  

---

## 📈 Business Impact
This analysis helps businesses:
- Identify high-risk customers  
- Improve retention strategies  
- Optimize pricing and service offerings  
- Enhance customer engagement  

---

## 📌 Conclusion
The project provides a complete end-to-end analysis of customer churn and delivers actionable insights to support data-driven decision-making.
